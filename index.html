About Me

I'm Bibek Bhattarai, a passionate and dedicated full stack software developer originally from Nepal, now based in Canada. With a strong foundation in Computer Science and Engineering from Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology in Bangalore, India, and currently pursuing a Postgraduate Diploma in Full Stack Software Development at Lambton College in North York, I’ve developed a deep understanding of both frontend and backend technologies. My journey into software development began with a fascination for how things work behind the scenes — that curiosity quickly grew into a purpose-driven passion for building web and mobile applications that solve real-world problems. I enjoy crafting user-friendly interfaces with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, while also developing robust backend solutions using Node.js, Express, and MongoDB. My projects reflect a blend of creativity, functionality, and scalability, and I’m always seeking new challenges that help me grow as a developer. In addition to technical skills, I bring strong communication, collaboration, and problem-solving abilities to the table, which I’ve developed through both academic work and real-world experience. I thrive in dynamic environments and believe in continuous learning — constantly exploring new frameworks, tools, and best practices to stay up-to-date in this ever-evolving field. Outside of coding, I enjoy content creation, cinematic video editing, and exploring new tech trends. My ultimate goal is to contribute to meaningful projects that make a difference, whether it's through innovative product development or impactful digital solutions. I'm excited about the future of technology and look forward to connecting with like-minded professionals and teams.
